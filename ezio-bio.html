Ezio Auditore da Firenze

Master Assassin and the Mentor of the Italian Brotherhood of Assassins

Here's a time line of Ezio's life:

  • 24/06/1459 Ezio was born in Firenze, as the second eldest child of Giovanni and Maria Auditore.
  • 28/12/1476 Ezio witnessed in horror as his father and brothers were executed.
  • 29/12/1476 The young man escaped his home city to protect his mother and sister and seek revenge from his family's killers.
  • 1477 He was trained to fight by his uncle Mario to kill his enemies and to join him later that year as an official member of the assassins's brotherhood.
  • 1488 The brotherhood aquared the apple of eden after Ezio defeated Rodrigo Borgia in the vatican.
  • 02/01/1500 The Papal forces led by Cesare Borgia besieged Monteriggioni, devastating the town and its Villa, while also killing Mario in the process and stealing the apple of eden.
  • 1506 The apple was retrived by Ezio and got hidden in the Colosium vault.
  • 1512 Ezio got married to Sofia Sartor in Constantinople.
  • 1542 Ezio auditorie Da Ferinse passed away after he got a heart attack.
